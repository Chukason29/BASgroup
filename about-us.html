What we are doing

We are re-inventing and democratizing access to investment capital, insurance, improving access to innovative financial services.

Established in May 2020 in Abuja, BAS Group is an investment advisory firm. We shape your portfolio to suit your investment style and objectives as well as effectively allocate your wealth to achieve expected returns.

BAS GROUP is a leading financial and investment advisory firm in Nigeria that provides customised investment support for individuals and corporate bodies through wealth planning, specialised investment solutions, and goal-based investing. With a proficient blend of experienced financial advisors, we provide our clients with sound and objective solutions to ensure consistent long-term investment growth.
